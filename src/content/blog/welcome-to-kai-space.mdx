Kai Space is a file-driven personal website template. Blog posts live in `src/content/blog`, subscription entries live in `src/content/subscriptions`, and server metadata lives in `src/content/servers`.

The project is designed for people who want one place for writing, private operational notes, and lightweight status panels.

<Callout variant="info" title="Template note">
Replace this post with your own writing after you finish configuring the site identity in `src/data/site.ts`.
</Callout>

## First steps

<Steps>
  <li>Copy `.env.example` to `.env` and set a password plus cookie secret.</li>
  <li>Run `pnpm install` and `pnpm dev`.</li>
  <li>Replace the demo content collections with your own files.</li>
</Steps>
